About Studio

who we are

ANA Interior Design LLC is a design & build company, involved in turnkey interiors fit out projects with well experienced professional team. At ANA, we offer our clients inspiring interior design concepts suiting their taste, lifestyle, and budget. We aim for excellence while constructing and delivering the projects. we design and develop high quality interiors and exhibition projects that create impact and exceed client expectations.

We have capability to carry out almost all trades of turnkey interiors, Joinery & MEP under one umbrella, thereby giving our customers edge of single point hassle free solutions and advantage over other fit out companies at competitive rates.
